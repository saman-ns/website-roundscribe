01 / The Problem

Nursing home rounding
was designed for one patient at a time.

Every other AI scribe assumes you're in a clinic — one patient, one recording, one note. But you round on fifteen patients before lunch. The tool needs to match the workflow.

01 /

15 patients before lunch

Each one needs a separate clinical note. The math doesn't work if you stop and restart recording between every room.

02 /

Notes wait until evening

Or Wednesday. Details fade, accuracy drops, and the documentation backlog becomes its own clinical risk.

03 /

Billing is an afterthought

OHIP codes are looked up manually, cross-referenced against the Schedule of Benefits, and frequently left on the table.

02 / How It Works

Record once.
Get notes for every patient.

The entire workflow happens automatically — from a single continuous audio file to structured, per-patient clinical documentation.
