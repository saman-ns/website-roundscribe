How It Works

01

Record

Open your phone, hit record, and round naturally. Talk to patients, nurses, and family — RoundScribe captures it all.

02

Transcribe

Medical-grade speech-to-text with speaker detection. Accents, medical jargon, and ambient noise — handled.

03

Segment

AI identifies patient boundaries automatically. "Moving to room 207" — new note starts. No manual splitting.

04

Generate

Structured clinical notes per patient, ready to paste into any EMR. Review, approve, done.
